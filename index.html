We are Justdiggit, the Nike of NGOs. We do first and talk later. Because we are in a hurry. Global warming is moving in fast. Our job is to slow it down, and we have one decade to do it. We believe in regreening degraded land, in cooling down the planet together and in the power of mother nature.

Just give her a little push and she will wow us with resilience and abundance. That’s why we go where we are needed most; Africa. We visit villages to spread the word and empower farmers to restore land. We combine traditional techniques with data and modern technology. And it is spreading like wildfire. We restored 60,000 hectares, brought back 5 million trees,

and built a grassroots movement that’s growing every day. Time to speed up and scale up! We are on a mission to regreen Africa in the coming 10 years, together with all 350 million farmers, and together with you. No more talking. No more waiting. This is the Decade of Doing. So dig in.
